🚀 Featured Project · Live
👑 Founder & Creator
Nyansa AI — Powered by Wisdom
nyansaai.netlify.app
View Live →

As the Founder and Creator of Nyansa AI, I built a learning-based AI platform for young Ghanaian learners — from Nursery through SHS — to make studying smarter, more accessible, and more engaging. The name Nyansa means "Wisdom" in Akan, reflecting the platform's mission to put powerful education tools in the hands of every child in Ghana and beyond.

The platform includes an AI Tutor that answers questions in English and six Ghanaian languages (Twi, Fante, Ewe, Ga, Hausa, Dagbani), an infinite Quiz engine across all GES subjects, an Essay Grader, AI-generated Flashcards, personalised Study Plans for WASSCE & BECE prep, a Live Classroom tool for teachers, a Parent Portal, and a Kids Mode with interactive alphabet and number learning for young children.

Nyansa AI is built with a deep commitment to inclusive education — featuring a dyslexia-friendly font option and larger text settings, ensuring no learner is left behind. It is a live, fully functional product and a testament to my belief that technology should serve communities, not just corporations.
